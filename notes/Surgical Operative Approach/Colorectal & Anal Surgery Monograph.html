Brutal Rule
Haggitt Level 4, Kikuchi Sm3, or LVI+ in a T1 polyp means the local resection was insufficient. You must proceed to formal oncological resection to avoid an unmanageable recurrence.

CME & TME Architecture

Page 4 / 15
2.1

Complete Mesocolic Excision (CME)

Principle: Intact mesocolic envelope + central vascular ligation. Dissection strictly in Toldt's fascia.
2.2

The "Holy Plane" of TME

  • Waldeyer's Fascia: Must be divided at S4 to avoid presacral haemorrhage.
  • Denonvilliers' Fascia: Dissect anterior to it for anterior tumours.
  • CRM: Positive if tumour ≤ 1mm from fascia.

TNT & Metastatic Trials

Page 7 / 15
3.1

The RAPIDO Trial

Short-course RT (5x5Gy) + Consolidation Chemo → Surgery. Resulted in higher pCR (28%) and lower treatment failure.
3.2

Lynch Tumour Algorithm

IHC for MMR → if MLH1 absent → BRAF V600E testing → if BRAF negative → Germline testing.
