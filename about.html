The Mission

The only resource built for the overnight shift.

Every surgical textbook is written for someone sitting at a desk with three hours and a cup of coffee. You are on hour sixteen of a call shift. You need the right answer now. That is why SIH exists.

01
Zero Paragraphs. Tables, bullets, numbered steps. If it can't be tabulated safely, it gets cut.
02
Current Guidelines Only. WSES, TG18, ASGE, SAGES. The exact year and version are explicitly cited.
03
Always Free. No paywall between a resident and the critical knowledge they need to treat a patient.
04
Written by a Surgeon. Not a marketing team. Not AI. A practicing surgeon who has stood where you are standing.
