About SIH

Built in the trenches.
For the trenches.

No academic institution funded this. No committee approved it. One surgeon decided that residents deserved better than what the textbooks were giving them.

The Founder
Dr. Abdulmalik Moh
Surgical Resident · Educator · @sugicalInsightHub

Built Surgical Insight Hub because the resources that existed were not built for how surgery actually works. Residents do not have time for narrative. They have time for decisions.

Every note has been written, reviewed, and tested against current guidelines by a surgeon who uses them clinically. Not adapted. Not summarised. Written from the call room at 3 AM.

The Mission

The only resource built for the overnight shift.

Every surgical textbook is written for someone sitting at a desk with three hours and a cup of coffee. You are on hour sixteen of a call shift. You need the right answer now.

01Zero paragraphs. Tables, bullets, numbered steps. If it can't be tabulated, it gets cut.
02Current guidelines only. WSES, TG18, ASGE, SAGES — year cited, version noted.
03Always free. Every note on Telegram. No paywall between a resident and the knowledge they need.
04Written by a surgeon. Not a content team. Not AI. A practicing surgeon who has stood where you are standing.
